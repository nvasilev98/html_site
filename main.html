Bizzare Cars

For its latest exhibit, the High Museum of Art in Atlanta has
assembled 17 of the most beautiful, hideous, and strange
automotive concepts ever dreamed up by man, from a 1934
Bugatti prototype up to an early iteration of
the Porsche 918 Hybrid supercar.

What happens when creators balance aesthetics, functionality,
and their personal vision of the future is one reason we love cars.
That is especially true when the results leave us wondering,
How could anyone think this was a good idea?
